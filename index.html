Visit Umatilla

Bask in nature within the small town known as the Gateway to the Ocala National Forest.

Top Three Activities to do in Umatilla

Explore the Springs

With over 3000 bodies of water in surrounding Lake County, there's plenty of options for the adventorous visitor.

Watch Local Sports

The local high school plays host to over 10 sports, including a playoff basketball team.

Eat Real Southern Food

Check out local favorites Haystax and the Mason Jar for authentic southern cuisine.
